ABOUT GENSHIN IMPACT

SETTINGS

Genshin Impact is set in the fantasy world of Teyvat, divided into seven distinct regions, each associated with an elemental Archon (god) and an element. Each region has its own culture, landscape, and challenges, inspired by real-world locations.

MECHANICS

The game features action-RPG mechanics where players explore Teyvat, solve puzzles, fight monsters, and complete quests. Players control multiple characters with unique elemental abilities, switching between them to create elemental reactions and defeat foes. Key mechanics include:

  • Elemental Combat System: Combining different characters' elements causes powerful reactions like Vaporize, Melt, and Superconduct.
  • Gacha System: Players unlock new characters and weapons through a "Wish" system, which resembles a lottery.
  • Exploration: The open world of Teyvat includes hidden treasures, puzzles, and dungeons, rewarding exploration.
  • Co-op Play: Players can team up with others to tackle bosses and dungeons.

THE MAIN STORY OF GENSHIN IMPACT

The main story of Genshin Impact follows the Traveler, a mysterious figure from another world searching for their lost twin sibling. Upon arriving in the world of Teyvat, the Traveler is separated from their sibling by an unknown god and loses their powers.

To uncover their sibling's whereabouts, the Traveler journeys across Teyvat's seven regions, each ruled by an elemental Archon (god). Along the way, they form alliances with locals, uncover the secrets of the Archons, and unravel the mysteries of Teyvat,
including its history and the forces threatening its balance.

The Traveler's journey intertwines with the fate of the world, as they confront larger threats like the Abyss Order and Celestia, while piecing together their own origins and purpose in Teyvat.

FUNFACTS IN GENSHIN IMPACT

Xiao's Mask Has Meaning

Xiao’s Yaksha mask is inspired by Nuo opera masks from Chinese culture. His mask also resembles a qilin, a mythical creature known for protecting the innocent.


Fischl’s "Ravenspeak" is Self-Taught

Unlike Collei (who can actually talk to animals with Dendro powers), Fischl’s "Ozian language" is entirely made-up by her. Oz just plays along.


Beidou Once Fought a Sea Monster Alone

Beidou killed the sea monster Haishan by herself using nothing but her greatsword and Electro Vision, earning the title "Uncrowned Lord of the Ocean."


Kazuha’s Vision Story is Unique

Kazuha’s vision didn’t come to him normally. It belonged to his deceased friend, and it resonated with him after their duel. His friend's willpower was so strong that the vision reignited after their death.
